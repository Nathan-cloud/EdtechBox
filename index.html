About Edtech box

Who are we ?

We are a non profit registered in the USA under Tech Box Education brand name. Our organization was established to democratize education in sub saharan africa rural areas where internet is inaccessible and inherently expensive. Ignoring the fact that internet penetration in Sub Saharan Africa is increasingly growing on a yearly basis, telecommunications companies committing massive investment in internet infrastructure, internet prices are not dropping thus increasingly becoming unaffordable to the majority.

The average poverty rate for sub-Saharan Africa stands at about 41 percent, and of the worldâ€™s 28 poorest countries, 27 are in sub-Saharan Africa all with a poverty rate above 30 percent. Projections by the World Bank also show that extreme poverty is showing few signs of improvement in sub-Saharan Africa, and may keep countries from ending extreme poverty by 2030. (Source: Poverty and Shared Prosperity: Piecing Together the Poverty Puzzle published by World Bank in 2018)

Why Education

One day, everyone in sub saharan Africa will have the opportunity to access all freely available digital educational content.
To build innovative tech products that democratize access to valuable educational content remotely by underprivileged communities, which hardly access the internet.

Edtech box develops, and supports a diverse network of leaders, working together to end educational inequity.

  • Equity:We work to avail freely available digital educational content to realize educational equity and excellence for all in Sub saharan Africa.
  • Impact: We pursue ambitious, meaningful outcomes that lead to access and opportunity for all in Sub Saharan Africa. We hold ourselves to high standards, make data-informed decisions, and orient to long-term success.
  • Humility:We acknowledge the limitations of our perspectives. We seek different points of view and historical context to evolve our thinking and actions.
  • Resilience: We see every challenge as an opportunity to think expansively about solutions. When faced with obstacles, we deepen our resolve, adapt, and persist with optimism.
